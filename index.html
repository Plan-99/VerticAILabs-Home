About VerticAI Labs

Democratizing Robot Learning for Everyone.

Our vision is to break down the barriers to robot learning. We are dedicated to creating intuitive software that empowers non-experts to teach robots a wide variety of complex tasks.

At VerticAI Labs, we believe that the future of robotics lies in its accessibility. We're building the tools to make that future a reality.

Our Product

Introducing 'Easy Trainer' - The intuitive platform for robot instruction.

Easy Trainer is a revolutionary software suite that allows users to train robots through simple, human-like instructions. No coding required. Just demonstrate, guide, and let our AI do the heavy lifting.

From manufacturing lines to research labs, Easy Trainer accelerates development and opens up new possibilities for automation. Beta access coming soon.
